### 📄 LiteRobustNet — Efficient CNN for Resource-Constrained Systems
> **Research Square (Preprint) · March 2026** &nbsp;|&nbsp; *Under Review at Springer — Neural Processing Letters*

<img align="right" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/L1R1tvI9svkIWwpVYr/giphy.gif" width="260" alt="Research GIF"/>

This research proposes **LiteRobustNet**, a lightweight and efficient CNN architecture designed for resource-constrained image recognition systems. Evaluated on **CIFAR-10** across baseline CNN, deep CNN, and optimized architectures — focusing on improving computational efficiency and robustness under hardware constraints.

**Key Contributions:**
- 🏗️ Novel lightweight CNN architecture for edge devices
- 📊 Comparative evaluation: Baseline vs Deep CNN vs LiteRobustNet
- ⚡ Improved computational efficiency without sacrificing accuracy
- 🔬 Robustness analysis under resource-constrained conditions
